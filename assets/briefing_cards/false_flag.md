# Lifeline: False Flag

## What It Does

A False Flag lets your team **disguise the attribution** of one of your
actions. When the round resolves, the world news feed will report the
action as if it was performed by the nation you chose to blame instead
of your own nation.

## How to Earn It

Solve an intel drop puzzle whose reward is a "false flag lifeline". Your
team receives one use per solved puzzle.

## How to Use It

1. Submit a proposal as normal (must still be in **draft** status).
2. In the proposal card, a "False Flag" section appears if you have
   remaining uses.
3. Select the nation to blame from the dropdown.
4. Click **False Flag** to attach it.

Once attached, the false flag is consumed and cannot be removed. The
blame attribution applies only if that proposal wins the vote and is
resolved.

## Strategic Notes

- False flags only affect **news attribution**, not the mechanical
  effects of the action. The target still takes damage from your team.
- Other teams with strong intelligence capabilities may suspect
  attribution anomalies.
- The UN Peace Council can still veto the underlying proposal.
