# Lifeline: Phone-a-Friend

## What It Does

Phone-a-Friend lets your team **peek at one enemy action** that has been
proposed (draft or locked) for the current round. You learn which nation
is planning what action and in which slot.

## How to Earn It

Solve an intel drop puzzle whose reward is a "phone a friend lifeline".
Your team receives one use per solved puzzle.

## How to Use It

1. In the **Lifelines** panel, click the **Phone-a-Friend** button.
2. The server randomly selects one enemy proposal from the current round.
3. An intel card appears showing the enemy nation's name, the action
   they are planning, and the slot number.

The lifeline is consumed immediately on click, even if no enemy
proposals exist yet (in that case you receive "No intel available").

## Strategic Notes

- Use this lifeline **after** other teams have had time to submit
  proposals for maximum value.
- The intel is a snapshot; the enemy team can still change their proposal
  before the round locks.
- Coordinate with your captain to decide the optimal moment to activate.
- Share the intel in team chat so everyone can factor it into voting.
